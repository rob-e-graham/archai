# AUXIO — A0 Street Poster Show (curated selection)

Curated 2026-07 for a public/street A0 poster show. Every object below is:

- **Print-clean** — CC0 / Public Domain / open (from Wellcome Collection, National Gallery of Art, and the Smithsonian), passing both `poster_download_allowed` **and** `media_canvas_safe`. No copyrighted game stills or street art.
- **High-resolution** — all carry a large/IIIF image derivative that holds up at A0 (841 × 1189 mm).
- **Live in AUXIO** — each has a generated visitor page (`nfc-pages/v/NFC<ID>.html`); the A0 export is produced from that page's *Save / print with QR* control.

Curated for a real mix of **modern + strange + eye-catching**, and for objects that reward a scan-and-talk conversation.

## Rights & provenance
All 15 are from **CC0 / Public Domain / open-access** programs (Wellcome, National Gallery of Art, Smithsonian Open Access) and pass the ingest print-eligibility gate. Rights line prints on every poster. This selection is legally clean to print, post, and distribute.

## To produce the A0 files
Each object's A0 poster is generated client-side from its AUXIO page:
`nfc-pages/v/NFC<ID>.html` → **Save / print with QR → A0**. The QR opens the live object conversation, so the street poster is also a doorway into AUXIO.
