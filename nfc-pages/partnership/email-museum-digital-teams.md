## Email body

---

Hi [Name / Digital Team],

I'm Rob, co-founder of ARCHAI — a platform that layers conversational AI onto museum collections using local language models, Qdrant vector search, and NFC/QR-deployable visitor pages.

I'm writing because [Institution] has been a significant source in our collection — we've been building on your open-access data [via API/GitHub dataset/open data portal], and we'd love to explore a more formal collaboration.

**What ARCHAI does technically:**

- Local LLM (Ollama / qwen2.5:32b or compatible) — no visitor data sent to third-party APIs
- Vector search via Qdrant — semantic queries across 20,000+ objects from 17 institutions
- Per-object AUX.IO pages: scannable via NFC or QR, conversational, mobile-first
- Downloadable posters at A4 / A2 / A0 / sticker / postcard with IIIF images and attribution
- Full technical documentation available — we support deployment and integration

**What we can offer your institution:**

1. **Live demo** of the visitor experience for your collection objects — we can show what your objects look like in the ARCHAI interface today
2. **Dedicated collection integration** — if you have data not yet in our pipeline, we can build a targeted harvester (following your licence terms exactly)
3. **Self-hosted pilot** — we can support your digital team in running ARCHAI on your own infrastructure for an internal research project or gallery pilot
4. **Physical deployment template** — NFC tag + poster system ready for a gallery floor or pop-up
5. **Data quality feedback** — harvesting your collection at scale surfaces interesting data quality signals we're happy to share back

**What's already working:**

We currently harvest and serve [17 institutions including The Met, Rijksmuseum, Tate, Wellcome Collection, Smithsonian, Getty, Te Papa, M+, Brasiliana Museus, QAGOMA, and others]. Every harvest respects the source licence exactly — CC0 for image display, CC BY with attribution preserved in payload, metadata-only where images aren't cleared.

**What we're not:**

We're not building a product to compete with your collection site. We're building infrastructure for the next layer — conversational, physical, deployable. We see ARCHAI as a supplement to your canonical record, not a replacement.

Would your team be open to a 30-minute technical call? Happy to walk through the architecture, show the visitor experience live, and talk through what an integration could look like for [Institution].

Best,
Rob Graham
ARCHAI — rob@fineartmedia.tech
fineartmedia.tech/archai / GitHub: rob-e-graham/archai

## Key messaging pillars for this track

1. **Data sovereignty** — local LLM, no third-party data flows, no visitor data leaving your walls
2. **Already using your data** — credibility through demonstrated use, not just pitch
3. **Technically serious** — Qdrant, Ollama, IIIF, NFC — speaks their language
4. **Licence-compliant by design** — legal harvest bot, per-item rights verification, exact terms followed
5. **Supplement, not compete** — positions ARCHAI as a layer on top of the institution's canonical record
6. **Reciprocal** — data quality feedback, co-authorship, research opportunities
